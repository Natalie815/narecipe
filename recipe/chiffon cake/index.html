Chiffon Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (281g) cake flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 7 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) cold water
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 7 large egg whites
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together cake flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add egg yolks, vegetable oil, water, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the egg whites into the batter until well combined.
  6. Pour the batter into an ungreased 10-inch tube pan.
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Invert the pan and let the cake cool upside down for about 1-2 hours.
  9. Once the cake is completely cool, run a knife around the edges of the pan and remove the cake.
  10. Serve and enjoy your chiffon cake!
